FAQ

What is the philosophy of Mojo?
Have a lean and efficient Companion to help you to fuel your growth; keep track of your habits and activities. All local at your phone, all protected, all private.

How does Mojo use your data?
You are the owner of your data. No personlized data sets are distributed or shared with us. It is all locally stored on your phone only. The Import/Export feature allows you to use your data as you wish.

Is Mojo Pro+ coming soon?
Yes! We are building a system to trade your Mojo for screen time. Stay tuned.
